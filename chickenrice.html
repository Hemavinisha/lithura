Chicken Fried Rice Recipe

  • Home

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup diced chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup peas
  • 1/2 cup diced carrots
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add diced chicken breast and cook until no longer pink.
  3. Add garlic, peas, and carrots. Cook for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Push the mixture to one side of the pan and pour beaten eggs on the other side. Scramble the eggs until fully cooked.
  5. Add cooked rice to the pan and mix everything together.
  6. Pour soy sauce and sesame oil over the rice and stir well.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Garnish with chopped green onions before serving.
